About

Welcome! I'm Andy Ennaco, Private Tutor and Apprentice Data Scientist. I've been enamored with Math & Science my whole life, and I have my family to thank for that. My father was an Engineer, and my mother was an AP Physics teacher until they both retired in the Spring of 2013. Of course, my older sisters (all 3 of them to be exact!) like to take credit for my mathematical acumen by recounting stories of playing school in our basement as kids, and teaching me how to add and subtract while I was still perfecting the art of walking and talking.
Years later, with my math fundamentals (and walking) mastered, I attended Simsbury High School, where I led the Math League to consecutive State titles my Junior and Senior years, and was awarded the RPI Medal for Math & Science. In 2007, I graduated top 10 in my class and attended Rensselear Polytechnic Institute in Troy, NY. There I received my Bachelor's of Science, as well as my Master's Degree in Mechanical Engineering, graduating in Spring 2013.
After graduating, I accepted a position as a Mechanical Design Engineer with Nerak Systems in Fishkill, NY. It was during this period that I began tutoring part-time. It didn't take long for me to realize that tutoring was a passion of mine. In the Spring of 2015 I packed up, moved to the Greater Boston Area, and ever since, I've been working with high school and college students to instill that same love for Math & Science I was so fortunate to discover as kid.
Now thoroughly I'm excited to begin the next chapter of my life as a Data Scientist!
